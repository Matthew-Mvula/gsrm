Spiritual Aspect


The ministry will aim at reaching out to the lost souls and renewing the spiritual man to be ready for the coming of Lord Jesus Christ through;




Outreach that will target large mass

Intercessions

Outreach in villages

Worship

Teachings on different topics to enhance spiritual growth


Family aspect


Family aspect will deal with families in these areas;


Preparing a youth to get right partner

Effective relationship that lead to a good family

Understanding the family and how to live a happy family

Productive family how to complement one another


Entrepreneurship aspect


These area will develop these concept;


Breaking a gap that is there between the fathers and sons on ministry of getting wealth

Impacting the young generation with skills of coming great entrepreneurs

Mentoring people on how to increase and start with what they have in business

Teaching on how effective money can be used to advance the kingdom


Mentorship Aspect


This concept links generations in these areas;


Receiving blessings from fathers of faith

Developing youth of substance through an example of people who have achieved in life.

Following their lives on how they handle family, finances, and their spiritual lives.


Compassionate aspect


The ministry will focus also on helping the needy in these areas;


Helping needy pastors without considering their religion affiliation

Assisting with knowledge and finances to the needy in any chosen community.
